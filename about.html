Our Story

Tomboy is a space for self-expression through style. We don’t follow labels — we break them. Whether soft or strong, subtle or loud — we believe every outfit should reflect your energy.

From clean cuts to bold colors, we design for the ones who dress for themselves. It’s not about fitting in — it’s about standing out.
